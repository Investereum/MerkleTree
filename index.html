We are experts in compliant Blockchain services

Merkle Tree specialises in transitioning utility token projects to compliant security tokens. Our service includes hands on delivery and execution, and covers all aspects of legal compliance, smart contracts, and marketing. Our expert team guide founders from end to end.

Our Best Services

In collabaration with a network of global experts, our services are broken down into the following categories

Legal

We set up your new entity and take care of all KYC & AML, governance and writing of the prospectus.

Finance

We advice you on your new token economics and make sure everything is tax compliant.

Technology

Based on the best technology for your solution, we develop the smart contract and process the token swap.

Marketing

Repositioning your solution to an STO will be driven by a smart social media and community strategy.

Delivery

Creating a regulatory interface for your STO. With possible customer, investor and/or on site support.

Growth

We help your business get to the next level with post STO services, business development or recruitement services.
